Who is Holly Hipwell?

Holly is an artist from Norwich, in Norfolk. She started doing life drawings whilst attending art classes at university. She has Friedrich’s Ataxia, but this doesn’t stop her determination to draw and paint. She gathers inspiration from Impressionist painters, as well as abstract work.

Holly explains: “I like to experiment with different mediums and explore new techniques. Art should be fun and expressive, not restricted by rules or boundaries. I always try to capture the viewer’s attention and imagination.”

Holly’s work is centred around large abstract canvases using mixed media and muted colours. She's happy to take commissions, or sell from her collection of already completed pieces.
